Luigi's Trattoria - Authentic Italian Restaurant in Downtown Chicago

Award-Winning Italian Cuisine Served Since 1985

Welcome to Luigi's Trattoria, downtown Chicago's premier destination for authentic Italian dining. For over 35 years, our family-owned restaurant has been crafting traditional Italian dishes using recipes passed down through three generations. Located in the heart of Chicago's Loop district at 123 State Street, we specialize in handmade pasta, wood-fired Neapolitan pizza, and classic Italian entrees prepared with imported ingredients from Italy.

Whether you're planning a romantic dinner, family celebration, business lunch, or special occasion, Luigi's Trattoria offers an intimate atmosphere paired with exceptional service. Our experienced chefs use only the finest San Marzano tomatoes, extra virgin olive oil from Tuscany, and aged Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese to create memorable dining experiences that transport you straight to Italy.

Signature Italian Dishes & Fresh Daily Specials

Our menu features authentic Italian cuisine prepared fresh daily with premium ingredients. Every dish showcases traditional cooking methods and bold, authentic flavors:

  • Linguine alle Vongole - Fresh littleneck clams sautéed with white wine, garlic, and Italian parsley
  • Margherita Pizza - Wood-fired pizza with San Marzano tomato sauce, fresh buffalo mozzarella, and basil
  • Osso Buco alla Milanese - Braised veal shanks served with saffron risotto and gremolata
  • Homemade Tiramisu - Classic Italian dessert with espresso-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream

Our pasta is made fresh every morning using traditional Italian techniques, and our pizza dough is fermented for 48 hours before being baked in our authentic wood-fired oven imported from Naples, Italy.
